Advocating for Safety in Civil Engineering

About

I am Rendell James J. Treyes, a second-year BSCE student. My advocacy aims to promote safety awareness and best practices in civil engineering, ensuring the well-being of workers and the general public in construction projects.

Core Ideas

Safety is a fundamental concern in civil engineering. My advocacy focuses on:

  • Implementing comprehensive safety training programs for workers.
  • Ensuring the use of personal protective equipment (PPE) at all times.
  • Adopting modern technologies to monitor and mitigate risks on-site.
  • Promoting adherence to local and international safety standards.
